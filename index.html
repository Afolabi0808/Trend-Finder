Money Map
Your Nigeria-specific path from zero to real income. No Western advice that doesn't apply here.
Phase 1 · Months 0–3
Build Foundation. Make Zero Naira.

This phase is about signals, not money. Train the algorithm, find your voice, build the habit. Most creators quit here — that is your advantage.

📱 Post Volume
4–5×/week. TikTok + Instagram Reels. Same content, both platforms. Phone + daylight + clean background.
Goal: 100 followers on each
💬 Active Engagement
Comment on 20+ posts daily in your niche. Reply to every comment. First 100 followers come from YOU, not the algorithm.
Goal: 5%+ engagement rate
🎬 Content Buffer
Shoot 5–7 cards when you have power and data. NEPA will not stop your schedule. Always 2 weeks ahead.
Goal: 2-week buffer always
📺 Open YouTube Now
Start the clock early. Post Shorts from your TikTok content. Watch hours accumulate while you sleep.
Goal: Channel live, 10+ Shorts
Phase 2 · Months 3–9
First Income: Affiliate + Brand Deals

Activate income streams that need no follower minimum and no platform approval.

🛒 Jumia Affiliate
Nigeria's largest e-commerce. Promote niche-relevant products. No follower minimum. Commission per sale.
₦20,000–₦150,000/month
📚 Expertnaire
Nigeria's top digital product platform. 50–80% commission on courses and ebooks. High ticket = high commission.
Top affiliates: ₦500,000+/mo
🤝 Nano Brand Deals
At 1K–5K followers, DM small Nigerian brands with your engagement stats. Local lifestyle, skincare, food brands.
₦40,000–₦150,000/post
🎁 TikTok LIVE Gifts
Go live 2–3×/week. Virtual gifts convert to real money. No follower minimum in Nigeria. Builds fast with consistency.
Grows with community
Phase 3 · Month 9+
Platform Revenue + Scale

YouTube AdSense unlocks. Passive income stacks on top of everything else. Brand deals grow with audience.

📺 YouTube AdSense
$1.50–$6.00 RPM for Nigerian creators. Creator Dyna Ekwueme earned $38,000 from YouTube. The most reliable long-term income.
Unlocks: 1K subs + 4K hrs
💼 Instagram Brand Deals
At 10K+ followers, macro deals open up. Nigerian macro influencers earn ₦700,000+ per post. Niche + engagement > raw numbers.
₦700,000+/sponsored post
🏪 Your Own Product
Digital products on Selar or Gumroad. You know your audience by now. Guides, templates, mini-courses. Unlimited ceiling.
You set the price. Fully yours.
📘 Facebook Reels
Opened to Nigeria 2024. Requires 5K followers + 60K minutes watched. Fourth income stream, same content you already make.
Requires: 5K followers
⚡ The honest truth

TikTok's Creator Rewards Program does not pay Nigerian creators. Use TikTok as a discovery engine, not a pay cheque.

YouTube is your most reliable long-term income platform in Nigeria. Start it now. Time in market matters.

Top earners stack multiple streams: affiliate + brand deals + YouTube AdSense + own product. Build toward all four.
